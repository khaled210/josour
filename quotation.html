We offer excellent and competitive rates to our clients. Our rates are determined by several factors

  • Our standard rate card. Call us for our updated rates and ask about your special discount if you are a first-time corporate user
  • The complexity and the nature of the text to be translated. More complex text like legal,engineering, medical requires more specialized resources and longer time to translate which translates to higher fees.
  • The timeline available for translation. High volume work in short time span may carry a surcharge as we ask our employees to work overtime in order to meet the constrains of our clients. In return, we like to be fair and generous to our employees and hence we pay them that overtime.
